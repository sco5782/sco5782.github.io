The Cast of Sanrio

By Sofia Orlando

What is Sanrio?

Sanrio is a Japanese company that specializes in making cute characters and merchandise. They were founded in 1960 and have been going strong, creating new characters, pop-up shops, and even amusement parks. Sanrio had such a big impact, not only did it shape Japanese culture, but American culture as well. You might have seen a cute little cat with a bow on clothes or makeup. That's Hello Kitty, Sanrio's most recognizable mascot! While she is the face of the company, there are many more characters who are just as cute. Here we will go over the most popular ones and showcase their different personalities and styles.

Meet Hello Kitty

  • Birthday: November 1st
  • Home: Suburbs of London
  • Star Sign: Scorpio
  • Favorite Treat: Apple Pie
  • Likes: Listening to music & eating apples
  • Hobbies: Baking & making new friends
  • Traits: Energetic, bright, & friendly

Character Information

Hello Kitty's design came about when Shintaro Tsuji, founder of Sanrio, began selling rubber sandals with flowers painted on them. He noticed that profits went up just by having a cute design on them, and hired Yuko Shimizu to create a character for his products. From there Hello Kitty became one of the most recognizable mascots in Japan. She has been featured in almost everything Sanrio-related, from merchandise to games, even to anime. She is good at baking cookies and loves Mama's homemade apple pie. She also likes to collect cute things and her favorite subjects in school are English, music, and art.

Meet My Melody

  • Birthday: January 18th
  • Home: Mariland Forest
  • Star Sign: Capricorn
  • Favorite Treat: Almond Pound Cake
  • Likes: Making gifts for friends
  • Hobbies: Baking & watching DIY videos
  • Traits: Honest, supportive, & caring

Character Information

My Melody debuted in 1975, one year after Hello Kitty. She originally appeared in a Sanrio Spin-Off telling of Little Red Riding Hood, where she played the main character. Her design originally had a red hood before settling on her classic pink a few years later. My Melody eventually became so popular that she even got her anime, Onegai My Melody. She loves baking cake and is honest to a fault. She is also Hello Kitty's best friend and lives in London as well.

Meet Kuromi

  • Birthday: October 31st
  • Home: Mariland Forest
  • Star Sign: Scorpio
  • Favorite Treat: Shallots
  • Likes: Romantic short stories & making mischief
  • Hobbies: Writing in her diary and singing
  • Traits: Villainous, tomboyish, & punk

Character Information

Kuromi originally appeared in the anime Onegai My Melody, as My Melody's rival and general antagonist. She was meant to contrast the very cutesy and girly My Melody and adopted a more punk aesthetic. Despite this, Kuromi is very feminie and enjoys writing and reading romance stories. She was often depicted as boy crazy and had many crushes during the run of Onegai My Melody. Kuromi also writes and sings songs that are officially made and produced by Sanrio themselves.

Meet Pompompurin

  • Birthday: April 16th
  • Star Sign: Aries
  • Fun Fact: Loves to collect & hide shoes
  • Favorite Treat: Cream Caramel Pudding
  • Likes: Watching pastry videos
  • Hobbies: Naps often & enjoys eating
  • Traits: Laid-back, curious, & generous

Character Information

Pompompurin desgin was pitched in 1996, when Sanrio held one of their many in house character desgin contests. His design was meant to look similar to purin, a popular Japense pudding desert. He ended up winning and Pompompurin debuted later that year in Strawberry News as My Pudding, with his name being changed Pompompurin a few monthes later. He's a laidback dog who loves soft plushies and easily makes friends. He can often been seen munching on sweet treats with pals and sleeping.

Meet Cinnamoroll

  • Birthday: March 6th
  • Star Sign: Pisces
  • Fun Fact: Can fly by flapping his ears
  • Favorite Treat: Cinnamon Rolls
  • Likes: Watching his favorite pop idols & dancing
  • Hobbies: Baking & sharing treats with friends
  • Traits: Shy, friendly, reliable

Character Information

Cinnamoroll was created by Miyuki Okumura for Sanrio in 2001. For the first year as a dog, he was named Cinnamon, but to not cause any problems with the registration of the trademark overseas, his official name became Cinnamoroll in 2007 and made his debut in the manga Fluffy, Fluffy Cinnamoroll. He works at Cafe Cinnamon and though he is quite shy, he likes to take naps on customers' laps. He also likes to fly around by flapping his big ears.

Meet Aggretsuko

  • Birthday: November 6th
  • Home: Tokyo
  • Star Sign: Scorpio
  • Favorite Treat: Beer
  • Likes: Yoga & Death Metal
  • Hobbies: Playing games & singing karaoke
  • Traits: Hardworking, responsible, & pent up

Character Information

Aggrestuko is a small red panda office worker who debuted in 2016. She is one of the few Sanrio characters to have an entire TV series dedicated to her and her story. Unlike other Sanrio characters, she is marketed toward adults and meant to represent the working class of Japan. She suffers from depression and stress from her office job and often goes out for death metal karaoke by herself to release her rage. Aggretsuko keeps this a secret from her co-workers as she views it as embarrassing or unladylike.
